Onboarding Your SolarEdge Inverter

Follow these steps to connect your SolarEdge inverter to the MicroGrid dGrid app.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Ensure Internet Connection

Make sure your SolarEdge inverter is connected to your home internet network. Refer to your SolarEdge inverter manual for connection instructions if needed.

2

Download the dGrid App

If you haven't already, download the MicroGrid dGrid app from the iOS App Store.

ðŸ“± Download dGrid App
3

Open the dGrid App and Register

Launch the dGrid app and follow the on-screen instructions to create your MicroGrid account. You will need to provide some basic information.

4

Connect Your SolarEdge Inverter

Within the dGrid app, navigate to the 'Connect Device' section. Select 'SolarEdge' from the list of manufacturers. The app will guide you through the process of linking your SolarEdge inverter using its serial number or other identification.

5

Verify Connection

Once connected, the dGrid app will display real-time data from your SolarEdge inverter. Verify that the data is accurate and up-to-date.

6

Set Your Preferences

Configure your energy sharing preferences and backup reserve levels within the dGrid app. You have full control over how your battery participates in the Virtual Power Plant.
