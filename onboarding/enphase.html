Onboarding Your Enphase Microinverters

Follow these steps to connect your Enphase microinverter system to the MicroGrid dGrid app.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Verify Enphase Envoy Connection

Ensure your Enphase Envoy gateway is connected to your home internet network and communicating with your microinverters. Check the Enphase Enlighten app to confirm all systems are online.

2

Download the dGrid App

If you haven't already, download the MicroGrid dGrid app from the iOS App Store.

ðŸ“± Download dGrid App
3

Create Your MicroGrid Account

Launch the dGrid app and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide basic information and verify your email address.

4

Connect Your Enphase System

In the dGrid app, select 'Connect Device' and choose 'Enphase' from the manufacturer list. You'll need your Enphase Envoy serial number and may need to authorize the connection through your Enphase account.

5

Authorize Data Sharing

Grant permission for the dGrid app to access your Enphase production data. This allows the app to monitor your solar generation and battery storage levels in real-time.

6

Configure VPP Settings

Set your energy sharing preferences, backup reserve levels, and participation schedule. You maintain full control over when and how much energy your system contributes to the Virtual Power Plant.
