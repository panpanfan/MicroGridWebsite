Onboarding Your Fronius Inverter

Follow these steps to connect your Fronius inverter to the MicroGrid dGrid app.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Verify Fronius Datamanager Connection

Ensure your Fronius inverter's Datamanager is connected to your home network. Check the connection status through the Fronius Solar.web portal or local web interface.

2

Download the dGrid App

If you haven't already, download the MicroGrid dGrid app from the iOS App Store.

ðŸ“± Download dGrid App
3

Set Up Your MicroGrid Account

Launch the dGrid app and complete the account creation process. Provide the necessary information and confirm your email address.

4

Connect Your Fronius System

In the dGrid app, select 'Connect Device' and choose 'Fronius' from the available manufacturers. Enter your Fronius inverter details including the serial number and Datamanager information.

5

Link Solar.web Account

You may need to authorize access through your existing Fronius Solar.web account to enable real-time data monitoring and control capabilities.

6

Configure VPP Participation

Set your preferences for Virtual Power Plant participation, including energy contribution levels, backup power requirements, and operational time windows.
