Onboarding Your SMA Inverter

Follow these steps to connect your SMA inverter to the MicroGrid dGrid app.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Check SMA Inverter Connectivity

Ensure your SMA inverter is connected to your home network via Ethernet or Wi-Fi. Verify connectivity through the SMA Sunny Portal or SMA Energy app.

2

Download the dGrid App

If you haven't already, download the MicroGrid dGrid app from the iOS App Store.

ðŸ“± Download dGrid App
3

Register Your Account

Open the dGrid app and complete the registration process. Provide the required information and verify your email address.

4

Add Your SMA Inverter

In the dGrid app, navigate to 'Connect Device' and select 'SMA' from the manufacturer options. Enter your SMA inverter's serial number and network details as prompted.

5

Authenticate Connection

You may need to provide your SMA Sunny Portal credentials or authorize the connection through your existing SMA account to allow data access.

6

Customize Your Participation

Configure your Virtual Power Plant participation settings, including energy sharing limits, backup power reserves, and operational schedules that work best for your household.
