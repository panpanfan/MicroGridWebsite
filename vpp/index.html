MicroVPP Grid Support Program — Participant Agreement

1. Parties & Purpose

This Participant Agreement ("Agreement") is between MicroGrid LLC ("MicroGrid," "we," "us") and you ("Participant," "you"). By signing below, you agree to enroll the home battery and solar system tied to your MicroVPP account ("System") in the MicroVPP Grid Support Program ("Program"). The Program aggregates many residential batteries into a virtual power plant that helps stabilize the local grid during peak demand and emergency events.

2. How the Program Works

  • MicroGrid issues dispatch signals to your System during grid-support events (a "Dispatch") that may discharge to your home loads, export to the grid, or charge from solar/grid as appropriate.
  • Dispatches only ever use the portion of your battery above the backup reserve you've already configured in your MicroVPP account. Our optimizer respects that reserve at all times.
  • Typical events last 30 minutes to 4 hours. The number of events per year depends on your utility territory and grid conditions.
  • You retain full ownership of your System. You may manually override or cancel any Dispatch from your inverter app at any time.

3. Authorization to Dispatch

You authorize MicroGrid (and its grid-services partners and utility counterparties) to send Dispatch commands to your System during the term of this Agreement, subject to the backup-reserve and override rights described above.

4. Compensation

MicroGrid will share grid-services revenue earned on your behalf, paid monthly via your selected payout method, subject to the rate schedule published in your MicroVPP account. Compensation depends on capacity offered, event participation, and program rules of the utility counterparty.

5. Data Access & Privacy

To run the Program, MicroGrid must read live and historical telemetry from your System (state of charge, power flows, site status, event performance). We use this data only to operate the Program, optimize your participation, settle payments, and meet regulatory or utility reporting requirements. We do not sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

6. Tesla Powerwall & the Tesla Fleet API

If your inverter is a Tesla Powerwall, MicroGrid uses the Tesla Fleet API to read your energy-site data and issue Dispatch commands. By granting Tesla Fleet API access, you authorize MicroGrid to (a) read energy device data for your Tesla energy site (energy_device_data), and (b) send energy commands such as backup-reserve and operation-mode changes (energy_cmds) during Dispatches. You can revoke this access at any time from your Tesla account at accounts.tesla.com.

7. Eligibility & System Health

You represent that you (or your household) own the System or have the legal authority to enroll it, that the System is in good working order, and that participation does not violate any agreement with your installer, lender, or utility. You agree to maintain reasonable connectivity (internet access to the System) so Dispatches can occur.

8. Term & Termination

This Agreement begins when you sign below and continues month-to-month. You may opt out of the Program at any time from your MicroVPP account or by emailing support@microvpp.com. MicroGrid may also terminate participation if a System becomes non-compliant or unsafe to dispatch.

9. Disclaimers & Limitation of Liability

The Program is provided "as is." MicroGrid is not liable for grid outages, lost backup capacity in extreme events, equipment wear within normal operating bounds, or revenue variation due to utility program changes. Our total liability under this Agreement is limited to compensation paid to you in the 12 months preceding the claim.

10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles. Disputes will be resolved in the state or federal courts located in San Francisco County, California.

11. Entire Agreement

This document, together with the MicroVPP Terms of Service and the Privacy Policy, forms the entire agreement between you and MicroGrid LLC regarding the Program and supersedes any prior understandings.
