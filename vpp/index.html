MicroVPP™ Grid Services — Customer Authorization

By clicking "Opt-In and Continue," you ("Customer") authorize the following:

1. Enrollment & management

You grant MicroVPP the exclusive right to automatically enroll and manage the dispatch of your battery and related energy assets in grid services and demand response programs. This authorization remains in effect until you revoke it. You confirm these assets are not, and will not be, enrolled in any other utility or demand response program outside of MicroVPP while this authorization is active.

2. Dispatch & control

You authorize MicroVPP and its authorized partners to monitor, control, operate, and dispatch your battery and related energy assets as needed to participate in applicable grid services and demand response programs.

3. Data sharing

You authorize MicroVPP, its authorized grid-services partners, your electric utility, and the applicable program administrators to access and use your energy usage, account, and device data — including interval and usage data from your utility's metering infrastructure, your name, service address, account and meter identifiers, rate plan, and device details — solely to enroll, operate, and settle your participation in these programs. Your data is never sold and is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable state privacy and utility data-access laws.

4. No fossil-fuel generation

You attest that you will not use diesel, natural gas, gasoline, propane, or liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) generation to deliver load reduction during any program event, where prohibited by applicable program rules.

5. Accurate information

You confirm that the information you have provided is accurate and complete, and you agree to notify MicroVPP of any material changes.

6. Revoking & opting out

You may revoke this authorization at any time by emailing info@microvpp.com, which ends your participation and stops future data sharing. You can also opt out of an individual dispatch event — just let us know — without revoking your overall authorization (frequent opt-outs may reduce your earnings or eligibility).

In plain English

Thanks for reading all the fine print — we know it's a lot. You're letting MicroVPP use your home battery to support the grid and earn you rewards, your data is used only to run the program, and you can opt out anytime.

How consent is captured

Customers complete an email opt-in, then affirmatively accept this authorization via click-through. MicroVPP records the acceptance for each customer.
