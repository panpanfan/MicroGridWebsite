What You'll Need

Before connecting your system, make sure you have the following information ready

🔌

Compatible Inverter

Your inverter must be from a supported manufacturer (Liniotech, EG4, Sol-Ark).

🌐

Network Access

Your inverter must be connected to your home network and accessible via its web interface or API.

📱

Mobile App

Download the MicroVPP™ dGrid app from the App Store to complete the connection process.

🔋

Battery System

A home battery system with sufficient capacity to participate in VPP programs (typically 5+ kWh).

📊

System Information

Basic details about your system including model numbers, capacity, installation date, and inverter web service credentials. Note: These are different from your consumer app logins - contact your installer for web service username/password.

⚡

Utility Connection

Your system must be grid-connected and in compliance with local utility interconnection requirements.
