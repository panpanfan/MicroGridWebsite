Step 2 · Authorize the Grid Support Program

📄 Customer Authorization

By checking the box below and clicking "Connect Tesla & Enroll," you ("Customer") authorize the following:

1. Enrollment & management

You grant MicroVPP the exclusive right to automatically enroll and manage the dispatch of your Tesla Powerwall and related energy assets in grid services and demand response programs. This authorization remains in effect until you revoke it. You confirm these assets are not, and will not be, enrolled in any other utility or demand response program outside of MicroVPP while this authorization is active.

2. Dispatch & control via the Tesla Fleet API

You authorize MicroVPP and its authorized partners to monitor, control, operate, and dispatch your Powerwall and related energy assets through the Tesla Fleet API as needed to participate in applicable grid services and demand response programs.

3. Data sharing

You authorize MicroVPP, its authorized grid-services partners, your electric utility, and the applicable program administrators to access and use your energy usage, account, and device data — including Tesla energy site and device data, interval and usage data, your name, service address, account and meter identifiers, rate plan, and device details — solely to enroll, operate, and settle your participation in these programs. Your data is never sold and is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable state privacy and utility data-access laws.

4. No fossil-fuel generation

You attest that you will not use diesel, natural gas, gasoline, propane, or liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) generation to deliver load reduction during any program event, where prohibited by applicable program rules.

5. Accurate information

You confirm that the information you have provided is accurate and complete, and you agree to notify MicroVPP of any material changes.

6. Revoking & opting out

You may revoke this authorization at any time by emailing info@microvpp.com or by revoking access in your Tesla Account, which ends your participation and stops future data sharing. You can also opt out of an individual dispatch event without revoking your overall authorization (frequent opt-outs may reduce your earnings or eligibility).

In plain English

You're letting MicroVPP use your Tesla Powerwall to support the grid and earn you rewards. We connect through Tesla's official Fleet API — we never see your Tesla password — your data is used only to run the program, and you can opt out anytime.

Step 3 · Connect your Tesla account

How the Tesla connection works. When you continue, we'll send you to Tesla's secure login at auth.tesla.com to authorize MicroGrid via the Tesla Fleet API (scopes openid, offline_access, energy_device_data, and energy_cmds). You'll return here automatically when you're done — it usually takes under a minute. We never see or store your Tesla password.

🔒 Checking the box is your electronic signature accepting the authorization. You'll be redirected to auth.tesla.com. See our Privacy Policy.
