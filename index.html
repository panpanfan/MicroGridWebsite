How It Works

Getting started with MicroVPP is simple. Follow these three easy steps to begin earning money while supporting your community.

1

Download & Connect

Download our dGrid app and connect your compatible home battery system. Our app will automatically detect your setup and guide you through the connection process.

2

Set Your Preferences

Choose how much energy you want to share and set your backup reserve levels. You maintain full control over your home's energy security.

3

Start Earning

When the grid needs support, your battery automatically shares excess energy. You earn money for every kilowatt-hour contributed to the network.
