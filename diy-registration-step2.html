📊 How to Find These Numbers (SCE Customers)

Open the "Your past and current electricity usage" section of your utility bill and look at "Your daily average electricity usage (kWh)". Copy each "This year:" value into the matching field above.

  • If you have rooftop solar: your bill shows two "This year:" values — one under Electricity Usage (net consumption) and one under Export Generation. Enter both.
  • If you don't have rooftop solar (or just installed it): enter the single "This year:" value as Net Usage and leave Export Generation blank.

Without system (one "This year:" value)

Example: Net Usage 24.10 kWh. Leave Export Generation blank.

With solar & battery system (two "This year:" values)

Example: Net Usage 23.20, Export 12.17 kWh.
