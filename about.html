About MicroVPP™

Our Mission

AI is reshaping how the world uses electricity — but for most homeowners, that means higher bills, not better outcomes.

At MicroVPP™, we believe AI's benefits should reach everyone. That's why we built an intelligent energy platform that helps homeowners unlock more value from their existing battery systems — automatically.

Our AI learns your energy patterns, predicts utility rates, and optimizes when to store or export power — helping you earn more from your battery and lower your costs, without lifting a finger.

Our mission: use AI to turn home batteries into smart, income-generating assets — making the AI transition rewarding for everyone.

Who We Are

We're a team of AI and energy engineers passionate about using machine learning to make home energy work for people. Our experience spans leading technology and energy organizations — including Microsoft, GE, and Hudson Sustainable Group — and decades of combined work in AI, clean energy, and technology.

We founded MicroVPP™ to make the AI revolution inclusive — to empower everyone.

Our vision is a future where every home participates, earns, and contributes to a smarter, fairer grid.
